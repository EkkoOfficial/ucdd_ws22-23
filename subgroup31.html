FindYourVibe - The quiz for finding bars, clubs and events in your local town!

Our approach

FindYourVibe is a quiz to find bars, clubs and events in a user's city.
The player can indicate what kind of bars, clubs and events he or she is looking for, according to his or her interests.
The quiz is the perfect way to get to know a new city, for example because of a move. Moreover, it is a convenient way to connect with like-minded people.
At the end of the quiz the user receives at least one suitable option that fits to his or her interests.
After that there is the option for him or her to join a forum.
There the user can create posts and reply to other community members' posts. The quiz is in German language because the Added Value Service is supposed to be integrated on a German community website.
